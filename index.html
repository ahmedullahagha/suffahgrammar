Core Academics

Fostering a Culture of Open Inquiry

Every subject is carefully sequenced to help students explore, question, and connect ideas — all while remaining anchored in faith.

📖

Structured Literacy

Grounded in the Science of Reading. Systematic phonics for early learners; knowledge-based schooling for advanced students to build analytical depth and vocabulary.

∑

Mathematics

Problem-based instruction (Illustrative Mathematics) with the CPA model. Students justify their reasoning and build conceptual rigour, not just procedural fluency.

🔬

Science

Phenomena-based and inquiry-driven, aligned with Next Generation Science Standards. The CER Framework ensures students construct explanations from data analysis.

💻

Computing & Design

True computational thinking through our iPad programme and Apple-powered tools. Students code, design, and innovate from the early years onwards.

Purposeful Design

For a Progressive & Holistic Education

Our school is shaped by carefully considered principles that place the learner at the centre of everything.

01

Active Engagement & Hands-On Discovery

Our curriculum moves beyond rote memorisation to prioritise inquiry-based learning — science experiments, collaborative projects, and real-world problem solving.

02

A Smart & Digital Ecosystem

Technology is woven into the fabric of our classrooms. A blended approach ensures students are fluent in 21st century tools, balanced with screen-free, hands-on activities.

03

Student-Centred Lesson Structure

The teacher is the guide; the student is the explorer. Our lessons foster independence and critical thinking — students analyse, question, and apply knowledge to the world around them.

04

Joyful Learning Experiences

School should be a place children run toward. We design our environment and schedules to reduce anxiety and spark wonder — because happy children learn best.

05

Tailored / Differentiated Instruction

We reject the one-size-fits-all model. Our teachers adapt methods to meet individual needs — whether a student needs reinforcement or advanced challenge.

06

Character & Community Values

Academic excellence is our priority; character is our foundation. We integrate integrity, empathy, and resilience into our daily routine — educating the heart as well as the mind.
